About This Dashboard

This dashboard visualizes solar panel power generation data collected every 10 minutes since March 2012. Use the controls above to explore different time periods and aggregation levels.

Daily View: Shows total kWh generated per day
Monthly View: Shows total kWh generated per month
Yearly View: Shows total kWh generated per year
