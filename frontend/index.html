About This Dashboard

This solar dashboard visualizes power generation data from March 2012 to present using an innovative annular (circular-to-line morphing) visualization.

Features:

  • Time Window Control: Adjust from 1 hour to 1 year of data
  • Multi-Year Overlay: Compare multiple years simultaneously
  • Interactive Legend: Click to highlight specific years
  • Amplitude Scaling: Auto-fit or use global maximum
  • Time Navigation: Precise control over date and time

Data Source: Solar panel generation data (kWh) collected every 5 minutes
